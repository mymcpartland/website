Dr. Mara Y. McPartland

Welcome. I'm a Postdoctoral Researcher working at the Alfred-Wegener Institute, Helmholtz Center for Polar and Marine Science (AWI), in Potsdam, Germany. I study past climate variability using tree-ring records, climate models and instrumental records. I received my PhD in 2022 in Geography from the University of Minnesota. My doctoral research focused the use of tree rings for studying complex relationships between forests and global change. I completed my Master's degree in Natural Resources Science and Management, also at UMN, studying the responses of northern boreal ecosystems global warming. I hold a Bachelor's degree from Bennington College, in Bennington Vermont.
